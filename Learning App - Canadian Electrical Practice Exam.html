What is the project?

The CSA Group is redesigning the current Canadian Electrician Practice Exam application. The redesigned learning platform has updated content from the Canadian Electric Code for Construction Electrician and Industrial Electrician with significant enhancements to the features. Our new platform is based on a web browser that is compatible to use on a laptop, tablet and, phone in English and French. Our product is curated with different study modes which supports the students to practice a full exam simulation similar to a 4 hour Red seal Exam and it also provides specialized study modes that help the students to concentrate on their weaknesses

Who are the targeted users?

Students and Junior Electrical Engineers who are preparing to write the Red Seal exams based on the Red Seal Occupational Standard for the trade to attain professional certifications. These students will be utilizing the product, 2021 Canadian Electrical Practice Exam to practice the complex and difficult technical examinations.
