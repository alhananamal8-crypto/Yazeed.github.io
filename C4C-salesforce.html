Salesforce Customer Portal Redesign

Hello there,

Due to NDA Non-disclosure agreement, please contact me for more in-depth information and data.

Overview

Centra for Customer Portal (C4C Portal) is built on Salesforce to store and process customer and technical data in the field of industrial certification. Our clients are B2B big electronic brands. The portal was built 3 years ago with no update the last 2 years.

My Role

I led the redesign of Centra for Customer Portal since customer service received many inquiries and complaints from our clients.

Our direct team has 1 UX designer (me), 3 developers and 1 Project manager.

Challenges

Understand the Exact Customer Needs due to the complexity of Electrical industry and Certification & testing standards. 80% of uses focuses on model certification.

The Approach

Our team decided to follow agile with small sprints and incremental validation pushed to customers until the pain points were addressed one by one.

The Discovery

Those methodologies were used:

Stakeholders interviews

Involve: Developers, customer service team, clients.

Why we choose it: Our clients are B2B leading electronics who have very unique insights about technical requirements. The previous version was designed 3 years ago without hearing the clients evolving needs.

Task Analysis

Why we choose it: A breakdown of the required information and actions is needed help designers and developers understand the current system and its information flows. This methodology makes it possible to allocate tasks appropriately within the new system.
