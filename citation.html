Citation Bank

  1. Product Design
  2. UX Design
Context

I have built “Citation Bank” as a project to submit for Adobe Jam Contest 2019. The topic is to create a mobile app that encourage academic integrity. My teammate and I wanted to create a new app to generate citations because there has been no big competitor in this field of mobile app yet.

My role

After the Adobe Jam contest, I decided to revamp the entire project, from Product design, UX research to UI visual design.

Motivation

I want “Citation Bank” to be the first big name as a citation generating app in the market. I am preparing pitch deck from this project and will propose to CitationMachine.net company.

Problem Statement

Many students simply just do not remember where they take their ideas from. Additionally, when students are skimming papers to get ideas for their articles, they are so lost in thoughts. The fact that you have to stop to take notes, then come back to reading can distract your focus. To tackle those two problems, "Citation Bank" is an one-stop converter and storage place for all of your reference.
Citation Machine and Turnitin are big names in term of academic integrity; however, they only support website format. There has been no direct competitor as a citation generating app yet.

Open cardsoting: I prepared about 40 cards with different keywords on it. The same 6 participants were asked to group those cards and give a name to each group.

Cardsorting response example #1

Design Process

Define problem

Firstly, many students are used to the habbit of asking their friends when they want to create citations. Other students generate citations on websites. To make Citation Bank become popular, I have to educate users the habbit of using mobile app to create citations.
Secondly, most people work on their academic papers on computer; hence it is more convenient to generate citations on websites like CitationMachine.net. Users can directly copy and citation to their paper works on computer. However, I will create functions like “Copy text”, “Save to library” and “Sync” so that users can still upload/sync citations on the computer after generating them on mobile app.
